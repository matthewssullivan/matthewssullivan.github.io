Hello and welcome to my website! Please pardon the mess, I've just started learning HTML this month and this website is my testground. As I'm always trying to broaden my knowledge base, I didn't want to use a website maker or Jekyll as a cheat (I apologize in advance for my taste in colors/themes).

By way of introduction, my name is Matt Sullivan and I'm currently pursuing my Masters of Computer Science at University of Colorado Denver (Class of 2020). I've worked in multiple industries in both the public and private sector, and while it took me a while to find my niche, I'm eager to catch up by learning all that I can about computer science. If you'd like to know more about me personally, please head over to the "About me" link or click here.

This website serves as a repository for my programming projects, both school assignments and personal. It also contains examples of my analysis and reports I have been assigned in classes throughout the last couple years. I hope you find it easy to navigate and that you enjoy the content. I will continue updating this website as I create new material (and as I can, class deadlines permitting).

Since this is my initial post, I'll keep a copy of it at the top. Further updates will work chronologically backwards from here. Who knows? With enough work I might actually get a scrolling blog going here. Maybe a few pictures to spruce up the place...
